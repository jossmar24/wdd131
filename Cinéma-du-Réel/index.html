Welcome to Cinéma Du Réel

Here I share some of my favorite documentaries and what makes them special to me. I hope you like them!

What will you find here?

  • My personal thoughts on documentaries that have left a lasting impression on me..
  • Analysis of cinematographic elements such as direction, acting and social and political issues to more personal and artistic explorations.
  • Movie recommendations for different tastes and moods.
  • Reflections on the impact of cinema on our culture and lives.

My passion for cinema

Documentaries have long been a gateway to new perspectives and a powerful way to understand the world. I enjoy exploring different themes and narrative approaches, always looking for revealing and thought-provoking films.

In this space, I hope to share my enthusiasm for documentaries and create a community where we can discuss and recommend inspiring films.

Latest Reviews

Succession

A documentary that explores the power dynamics and infighting within a wealthy family, focusing on the dispute for control of a family business empire. Through an intimate and observational narrative, the film offers deep insight into family relationships, personal ambitions, and the tensions that arise when company leadership is at stake.

Vourdalak

a film that offers a unique and stylistically distinctive vision of the vampire myth, inspired by the novel of the same name by Aleksey Konstantinovich Tolstoy. Directed by Adrien Beau, the film is set in the 18th century and follows Marquis Jacques Antoine Saturnin d'Urfé (played by Kacey Mottet Klein), a French aristocrat who, after being robbed by bandits, seeks refuge in the home of a rural family in Eastern Europe. There, he falls in love with Sdenka (Ariane Labed), the daughter of the family.
