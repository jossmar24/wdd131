Website Planning

Site Name

Cinéma du Réel

The name "Cinéma du Réel" translates from French as "Cinema of the Real" or "Cinema of Reality." It represents the essence of the festival, which focuses on documentary film and exploring reality from different cinematographic perspectives.

Site Purpose

The page functions as an information and dissemination center for filmmakers, critics and documentary lovers, promoting real cinema internationally.

Scenarios

  • Is there an archive of previous editions with access to documentaries and conferences?
  • Does the festival organize round tables or debates on documentary film?

Color Schema

  • Black (#000000): Main background or featured sections (such as banners and headers). It represents elegance, classic cinema and depth.
  • White (#FFFFFF): Main text to improve readability on dark backgrounds, it symbolizes clarity, transparency and professionalism.
  • Dark gray (#333333): Secondary funds and informative sections, it gives a modern and serious tone without being as harsh as pure black.

Typography

  • Headings: Montserrat
  • Body: Open Sans
